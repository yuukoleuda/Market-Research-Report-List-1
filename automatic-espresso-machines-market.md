<p><p>The automatic espresso machine market primarily includes semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. Semi-automatic espresso machines offer users control over the brewing process by requiring manual input for grinding and tamping, allowing for greater customization. In contrast, fully automatic machines automate the entire espresso-making process, including grinding, brewing, and frothing, appealing to those seeking convenience and consistency. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing manual engagement with automation in the espresso preparation experience.</p></p>
