Soil inoculants involve similar microorganisms introduced directly into the soil to improve nutrient availability, soil health, and crop productivity. Both markets play a crucial role in sustainable agriculture, promoting ecological balance and increasing crop yield while reducing reliance on chemical fertilizers and pesticides, thus supporting thriving agricultural ecosystems.</p></p>
