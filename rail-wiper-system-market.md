<p><strong>The Rail Wiper System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pendulum Systems</li><li>Dual Pendulum Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail wiper system market is primarily categorized into single pendulum systems and dual pendulum systems. Single pendulum systems utilize a single arm that sweeps across the train windows to remove debris, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity. In contrast, dual pendulum systems employ two arms for a more comprehensive cleaning action, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness against tougher contaminants. Both types are essential for maintaining visibility and safety in rail operations, catering to different operational and budgetary needs.</p></p>
