<p><p>The EVA geomembrane market is categorized into three main types based on layer configurations. Single-layer geomembranes consist of a single sheet of ethylene-vinyl acetate, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Double-layer geomembranes feature two layers that enhance durability and resistance to punctures, suitable for more demanding applications. Three-layer geomembranes incorporate an additional layer for further strength and chemical resistance, often used in complex environmental projects and waste management. Each type serves specific needs in containment and environmental protection.</p></p>
