<p><p>The automotive seating system market includes various types designed for different vehicle configurations and passenger needs. </p><p>**Bench seats** offer a continuous seating space, ideal for maximizing passenger capacity, commonly found in trucks and vans. </p><p>**Split seats** feature individual sections, allowing for varied adjustments and enhanced comfort, often seen in sedans and SUVs. </p><p>**Split bench seats** combine the benefits of both, providing a single bench design with the ability to fold down individual sections for flexible cargo and seating options. Each type serves distinct practical and stylistic purposes in vehicle design.</p></p>
