<p><p>The PCB board market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Multilayer PCBs contain multiple layers of circuitry, allowing for compact designs in complex devices. High-Density Interconnect (HDI) boards offer finer lines and more features in a smaller footprint, enhancing performance in advanced electronics.
