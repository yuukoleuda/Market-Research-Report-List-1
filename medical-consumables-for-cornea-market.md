<p><p>The medical consumables for cornea market includes various products essential for corneal procedures. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are implanted during cataract surgery to replace the eye's cloudy natural lens, improving vision. Artificial corneas, or keratoprostheses, serve as implantable devices for patients with severe corneal damage who cannot benefit from traditional grafting methods. Both IOLs and artificial corneas play crucial roles in enhancing patient outcomes and vision restoration, addressing prevalent eye conditions related to corneal health.</p></p>
