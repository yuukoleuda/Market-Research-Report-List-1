<p><strong>The Recycled (Retreaded) Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled (retreaded) tyres market primarily consists of two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-manufactured tread onto the cleaned and prepared tyre casing, allowing for efficient production. In contrast, mold cure retreading entails the use of a new tread that is heated and molded directly onto the tyre casing, resulting in a more uniform finish. Both methods extend the tyre's lifespan, reduce waste, and provide cost-effective solutions, catering to various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
