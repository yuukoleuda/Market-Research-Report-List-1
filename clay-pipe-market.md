<p><strong>The Clay Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glazed</li><li>Unglazed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clay pipe market can be categorized into glazed and unglazed types. Glazed clay pipes have a smooth, sealed surface that enhances durability and resists corrosion, making them suitable for wastewater and stormwater applications. In contrast, unglazed clay pipes are porous, allowing for natural drainage, often used in agricultural drainage systems. Each type serves distinct functions, with glazed pipes favored for their longevity and maintenance ease, while unglazed pipes are valued for their permeability and cost-effectiveness in specific environments.</p></p>
