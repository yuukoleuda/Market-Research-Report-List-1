<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) services encompass three main types: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM is a process that uses a shaped electrode to create intricate details in materials. Wire EDM employs a thin wire as an electrode to cut precise shapes, ideal for complex geometries. Hole Drilling EDM specializes in creating precise holes in hard materials, often used for cooling holes in turbines. These methods are vital for manufacturing components with high accuracy and intricate designs.</p></p>
