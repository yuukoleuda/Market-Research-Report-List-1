<p><p>The double-acting rotary actuator market includes various types, notably balanced rotary actuators and rack-and-pinion pneumatic rotary actuators. Balanced rotary actuators provide consistent torque output in both directions, enhancing efficiency and precision in applications. Rack-and-pinion pneumatic rotary actuators utilize a gear mechanism to convert linear motion into rotary motion, offering high torque and rapid response. Both types are essential in automation and control systems, enabling precise movement in industrial processes and equipment.</p></p>
