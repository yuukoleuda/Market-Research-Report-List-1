<p><p>The anticoagulant drugs market consists of several key types: NOACs (Novel Oral Anticoagulants), Heparin, Warfarin, and Others. NOACs, such as rivaroxaban and apixaban, offer advantages like fixed dosing and fewer dietary restrictions. Heparin, typically administered via injection, is commonly used for immediate anticoagulation in hospital settings. The "Others" category includes synthetic and herbal anticoagulants, expanding treatment options for managing thromboembolic disorders.</p></p>
