<p><strong>The MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Superconducting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI market is primarily segmented into permanent magnet and superconducting systems. Permanent magnet MRI machines utilize fixed magnets to generate consistent magnetic fields, making them cost-effective and portable, suitable for clinics with limited space. In contrast, superconducting MRI systems employ cooled superconducting magnets, allowing for stronger magnetic fields that enhance imaging resolution and quality, ideal for larger hospitals and specialized facilities. Each type serves different medical needs and operational capacities within the healthcare landscape.</p></p>
