<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) controllers market comprises several types, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, ZigBee, and others. Wi-Fi IoT controllers facilitate high-speed internet connectivity, making them ideal for devices needing extensive data exchange. Bluetooth controllers enable short-range communication, often used in personal devices. ZigBee controllers focus on low-power, low-data-rate applications, suitable for home automation and industrial uses. Other types may include protocols like LoRaWAN or NFC, catering to specific needs and varying ranges in connectivity and power consumption.</p></p>
