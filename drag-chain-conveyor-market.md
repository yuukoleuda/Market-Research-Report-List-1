<p><ul><li>Standard Drag Chain Conveyors</li><li>En-masse Drag Chain Conveyors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drag chain conveyor market comprises two primary types: standard drag chain conveyors and en-masse drag chain conveyors. Standard drag chain conveyors are designed for transporting bulk materials using a series of linked chains, suitable for moderate loading applications. In contrast, en-masse drag chain conveyors utilize a unique design that allows for efficient movement of bulk materials by moving them in a dense formation, making them ideal for high-capacity applications and minimizing space requirements.</p></p>
