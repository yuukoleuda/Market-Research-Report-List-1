<p><strong>The Non-invasive Breathing Technological Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive-pressure</li><li>Negative-pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-invasive breathing technology market comprises two main types: positive-pressure and negative-pressure systems. Positive-pressure devices, such as CPAP machines, deliver air to keep the airways open, primarily used for conditions like sleep apnea. In contrast, negative-pressure systems create a vacuum around the chest to assist with lung expansion, often utilized in conditions like respiratory muscle weakness. Both types aim to enhance respiratory function without the need for invasive procedures, catering to various patient needs and improving overall respiratory health.</p></p>
