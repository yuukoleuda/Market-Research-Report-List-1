<p><p>The bolted cover piston check valve market is primarily segmented into two types: thread type and socket weld type. Thread type valves feature threaded connections, allowing for installation in tight spaces and easy maintenance, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Socket weld type valves, on the other hand, are designed for permanent connections, providing robust strength and leak resistance, ideal for high-pressure systems. Both types serve critical roles in preventing backflow in various industrial applications, showcasing their specific advantages based on installation needs.</p></p>
