<p><strong>The Disposable Hemodialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Flux Dialyzer</li><li>Low-Flux Dialyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable hemodialyzer market is categorized into high-flux and low-flux dialyzers. High-flux dialyzers are designed with larger pores, allowing for the efficient removal of larger molecules and toxins, making them suitable for patients with higher metabolic waste. In contrast, low-flux dialyzers have smaller pores, primarily targeting smaller solutes, which may be adequate for less complex cases. The choice between these types depends on patient needs, treatment objectives, and specific clinical conditions in renal therapy.</p></p>
