<p><p>The transparent heating film market is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer films consist of a single conductive layer that provides efficient heating, making them suitable for simpler applications. Multi-layer films, on the other hand, incorporate multiple conductive and insulating layers, enhancing durability and thermal efficiency. This layered structure enables better heat distribution and transparency, making them ideal for advanced applications like smart windows and automotive displays, where aesthetic appeal and functionality are crucial.</p></p>
