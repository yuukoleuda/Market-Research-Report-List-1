Liquid Nitrogen systems, on the other hand, offer rapid cooling capabilities, enhancing the efficacy of the ablation process. Each type is designed to improve patient outcomes by targeting specific cardiac arrhythmias with minimal collateral damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
