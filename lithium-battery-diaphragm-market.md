<p><strong>The Lithium Battery Diaphragm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator</li><li>Bilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator</li><li>Trilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium battery diaphragm market comprises three main types of separators: monolayer, bilayer, and trilayer lithium-ion battery separators. Monolayer separators consist of a single layer of material providing basic insulation and ionic conductivity. Bilayer separators enhance performance by utilizing two layers, improving thermal stability and reducing the risk of short circuits. Trilayer separators, featuring three layers, offer superior safety and efficiency, enabling higher energy densities and prolonged battery life. Each type serves specific applications and performance requirements in the battery industry.</p></p>
