<p><p>The hybrid floral seed market encompasses three primary types: annual, biennial, and perennial seeds. Annual seeds complete their lifecycle in one growing season, offering vibrant blooms and quick turnover. Biennial seeds require two years to mature, providing blossoms in the second year, which allows for seasonal variety. Perennial seeds thrive for multiple years, returning year after year, making them a favorite for stable gardens. Each type serves unique horticultural needs and preferences, contributing to diverse floral displays.</p></p>
