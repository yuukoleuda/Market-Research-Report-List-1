<p><p>The alarm detector market is divided into two primary types: active and passive detectors. Active detectors emit a signal and monitor the environment for any changes that indicate an alarm condition, such as smoke or motion. In contrast, passive detectors rely on sensing changes in the environment without generating a signal themselves, typically detecting heat, sound, or light emissions from sources like fire or intruders. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing security and safety across various applications.</p></p>
