<p><strong>The Polyimide Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide coatings are categorized into two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset polyimides, once cured, become stable and cannot be remolded, making them suitable for high-temperature applications and providing excellent chemical resistance. In contrast, thermoplastic polyimides can be reprocessed and reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and ease of application. These coatings are widely used in aerospace, electronics, and automotive industries due to their superior thermal stability, mechanical strength, and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
