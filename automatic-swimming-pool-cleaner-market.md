<p><strong>The Automatic Swimming Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic swimming pool cleaner market primarily features two drive types: crawler drive and wheel drive. Crawler drive cleaners use a track system, offering superior maneuverability and traction on various surfaces, making them ideal for uneven pool bottoms. Wheel drive cleaners, on the other hand, utilize wheels for movement, providing efficient navigation on flat surfaces and speed in cleaning. Both types enhance pool maintenance, but their effectiveness can vary based on the pool's shape and the type of debris present.</p></p>
