<p><p>The aluminum beverage can market is primarily divided into two types: pull-tab and stay-on-tab cans. Stay-on-tab cans, on the other hand, have a hinge mechanism that keeps the tab attached after opening, enhancing convenience and reducing litter. Both types cater to consumer preferences, with stay-on-tabs increasingly favored for their sustainability and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
