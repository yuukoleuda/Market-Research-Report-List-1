<p><p>Termite baiting systems are categorized into three main types based on the termite species targeted. Subterranean termite baiting systems focus on controlling underground termites, offering a preventive and curative approach. Dampwood termite baiting systems target termites that thrive in damp conditions, typically found in wood with high moisture content. Drywood termite baiting systems are designed for termites that live within the wood itself, requiring specialized bait formulations for effective control. Each type serves distinct ecological niches and pest management strategies.</p></p>
