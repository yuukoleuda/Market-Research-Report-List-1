<p><p>The manual tissue paper dispenser market is primarily divided into two types: surface-mounted and countertop-mounted dispensers. Countertop-mounted dispensers are designed for placement on counters or tables, commonly used in kitchens, cafes, and offices, offering flexibility and easy access for users. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, emphasizing functionality and space efficiency in diverse environments.</p></p>
