<p><p>In a House and Equipment Online Auction Platform, the market types are categorized into reserve price and no reserve price auctions. A reserve price auction sets a minimum price that must be met for the property or equipment to sell, ensuring the seller's expectations are protected. Conversely, a no reserve price auction allows the item to be sold to the highest bidder, regardless of the final bid amount, often attracting more bidders and potentially driving prices up.</p></p>
