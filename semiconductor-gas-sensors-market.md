<p><p>The semiconductor gas sensors market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I sensors typically utilize metal oxide semiconductor (MOS) technology, which detects gases through changes in electrical conductivity when exposed to specific analytes. These sensors are widely used for indoor air quality monitoring and industrial applications. On the other hand, Type II sensors often incorporate advanced materials like graphene or carbon nanotubes, offering higher sensitivity and faster response times, making them suitable for more demanding applications such as environmental monitoring and safety systems.</p></p>
