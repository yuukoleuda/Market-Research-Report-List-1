<p><p>The electromagnetic metamaterial market is categorized into three main types: absorbing materials, shielding materials, and others. Absorbing materials are designed to minimize electromagnetic wave reflection, enhancing stealth and reducing interference in various applications. Shielding materials aim to block or attenuate unwanted electromagnetic fields, protecting sensitive electronics and ensuring signal integrity. The "others" category includes materials with unique properties or specific applications, such as frequency-selective surfaces or superlenses, catering to niche requirements in telecommunications, defense, and medical fields.</p></p>
