<p><ul><li>Naphthenic Base</li><li>Paraffin Base</li><li>Aromatic Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber filling oil market comprises three main types: naphthenic, paraffin, and aromatic bases. Naphthenic base oils are valued for their excellent solubility and elasticity, making them ideal for tire manufacturing. Paraffin base oils offer good stability and low volatility, enhancing durability in rubber products. Aromatic base oils provide superior processing characteristics and compatibility with various rubber compounds. Each type brings distinct properties that cater to specific performance requirements in the rubber industry, influencing application and market dynamics.</p></p>
