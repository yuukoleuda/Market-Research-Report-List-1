<p><p>The Negative Feedback Transistor Linear Regulator IC market consists of two primary types: Series Type and Shunt Type. Series Type regulators maintain a constant output voltage by adjusting the resistance in series with the load, effectively controlling the output voltage based on feedback from the output. Conversely, Shunt Type regulators operate by diverting excess current away from the load, maintaining the desired voltage through a feedback mechanism. Both types ensure stable voltage regulation, catering to different application requirements in electronic circuits.</p></p>
