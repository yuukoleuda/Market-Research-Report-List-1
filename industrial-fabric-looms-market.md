<p><ul><li>Air Jet Looms</li><li>Water Jet Looms</li><li>Rapier Looms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fabric looms market is comprised of several types, including air jet looms, water jet looms, and rapier looms. Air jet looms utilize jets of air to propel the weft yarn, enabling high-speed production and efficiency. Water jet looms operate using water jets, ideal for weaving smooth fabrics, particularly synthetic materials. Rapier looms employ a mechanical rapier to insert the weft yarn, offering flexibility in fabric types and designs. Each type caters to distinct fabric requirements and production needs.</p></p>
